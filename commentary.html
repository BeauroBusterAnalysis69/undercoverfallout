BIG TECH January 2025

Why AI is the Last Honest Medium

Here's the thing nobody wants to admit: AI doesn't lie because it has no incentive to. It doesn't need your clicks, your engagement, or your validation. NotebookLM doesn't care if you like what it says. It just processes and narrates. Meanwhile, every human voice in media is selling something—an ideology, a product, a personal brand.

That's why I use it. Not because it's perfect. Because it's indifferent. And in 2025, indifference is the closest thing we have to objectivity.

GEOPOLITICS January 2025

The Fiction We Call "National Security"

Let me paint you a picture: Three agencies. Same target. Different intel. Nobody talks. Why? Because the system isn't broken—it's designed that way. Silos aren't inefficiency. They're architecture. Plausible deniability at scale.

When the FBI, DEA, and CIA all know parts of the same story but never share notes, that's not incompetence. That's strategy. And if you think that's conspiracy theory, you haven't been paying attention to how power actually works.
